Welcome to VJTI OLX

Your One-Stop for Notes and Exam Preparation

What and whom is this website for?

This website is created for students who would like to become millionaires after selling their notes. (Just kidding). The purpose of this website was to motiavte positive interaction between the students of our college in terms of academics. We all know the guy, who works hard, takes notes and gets the best package. Well their hard work should not go in vain, and somebody else could benefit from his studies. So, this website allows students to upload notes and also allows access to those notes. Well, this is a relief, so dont't worry if you haven't taken studies, we got you covered!

Find the right notes for you

Our website has curated notes ranging from students of First Year to the Final Year in PDF format. You can select the right notes for your exam preparation, by navigating through reviews of each of the notes and also review in the view-only panel of the website.

How Do I work through this site?

Steps to follow:
  • Create an ID on the website using your VJTI College ID by signing through G-Mail.
  • Select your specific year and complete the registration.
  • Now you're good to go - just use the search bar to access the uploaded notes and study materials on the website.




This website is only created for the students of Veermata Jijabai Technological Institute. No other college student is given the right to access the material in this website.
